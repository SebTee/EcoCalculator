• FAQ

    General
  • How do I sign up?

    You can sign up by clicking here!

  • Do I have to take the questionnaire every time?

    Yes, as your answers may differ each time you access the app. After completing this stage, the app will then offer you suitable recommendations, advising you on the next steps you can take to improve your carbon footprint, based on the answers you provide.

  • How accurate are the results?

    The results produced by the app provides a basic idea on the damage an individual has caused on the environment. The results are separated into four categories: transport, shopping, energy use and food. Each category will be assigned a percentage value and according to these values, it will produce appropriate recommendations.

  • Is this app free?

    Yes! Eco-Calculator is free to use for both registered and guest users!

  • Is this compatible with all browsers?

    Yes, this app functions on Google Chrome, Microsoft Edge, Firefox, and etc.
